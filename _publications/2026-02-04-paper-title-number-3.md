Neural circuits are key to understanding brain connectivity, showing how stuctural pathways support brain function. While diflusion MRltractograply can accurately and consistently map these circuit, defining them from complex fiber data remains a significant challenge.We aim to identify the white-matter neural circuit using diffusion MRI tractography to link cognitive functions to brain mechanisms.Using a comprehensive ensemble tractography, we developed a cluster-level, parcellation-based connectome identification method tocharacterize the connectome within the white-matter neural circuit.We produced a high-fidelity atlas of consistent fiber-cluster-based neural circuits that robustly represent the brain's structural connectivitybetween major cortical and subcortical regions.
